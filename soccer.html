Spring Soccer 2024 Season Prep

We want to start the spring season in a good form. During winter months if you do not practice, you will slump and forget what you were doing so well in the fall. By doing even a few moves twice a week you will keep your feet engaged and be ready to play well in the spring. Please use the Spring Season prep sheet.
